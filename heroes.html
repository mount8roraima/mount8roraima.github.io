What would the lives of us mere mortals be without our larger-than-life heroes?

A hero is more than a sandwich. A real hero can provide us with hope in the

darkest moments in our dull and dreary lives. A hero can bring color to our

bland existences and give us a new day to which we can look forward.

A hero can lift up our spirits and dash the contrivances of that which

is “not so good” These postage stamps reveal some of my heroes.
